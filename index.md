# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-22

## Overview
This app allows users to connect their WHOOP account to view selected fitness metrics inside the app.

## Data We Collect
- We store WHOOP OAuth tokens (access token and refresh token) to access your WHOOP data after you authorize the connection.
- We may store selected WHOOP metrics (e.g., sleep, recovery, workouts) to display them in the app.

## How We Use Data
- To fetch and display your WHOOP metrics in the app.
- To keep your WHOOP connection working (refresh tokens when needed).

## Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as required to provide the WHOOP integration.

## Security
We take reasonable measures to protect stored tokens and data. You can disconnect WHOOP at any time.

## Your Choices
- You can disconnect WHOOP from the app, which stops future data access.
